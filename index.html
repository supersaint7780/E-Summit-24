ABOUT US
Entrepreneurship Cell NIT Bhopal is a non-profit student organization that works to support and unleash the budding entrepreneurial minds and promote the start-up culture among the students.
E-Summit is the flagship Event of Entrepreneurship Cell MANIT, aimed at fueling the entrepreneurial spirit in the students, held start-up founders, and educating the inquisitive minds to create an innovation driven start-up culture. We aim to bring together the academic community, venture capitalists, new-age entrepreneurs and all those passionate about making a difference to common grounds and to give a chance to the students to exhibit and develop their entrepreneurial talent and creativity through various business related games, workshops, guest lectures and competitions; and to create an environment for everyone to learn and grow.

Join Events

What’s in the box?

Start Up Expo

Start-up Expo serves as a medium for all the start-ups aiming to soar high. This event brings the opportunity to present your ideas before investors and procure investments, founders and working space from experts.

Business Plan Competition

The event provides opportunity to the start-ups in ideation and validation phase to pitch their ideas in front of a bench of venture capitalists and a panel of experts in order to secure funding.

Case Study Competiton

This competition, in collaboration with a start-up or established company, challenges participants with real-world business problems. They present their solutions to a panel, fostering critical thinking and nurturing an entrepreneurial mindset.

Hackathon

Central India’s largest hackathon entails participants to rack their grey cells to think and code solving real world problem sets with the help of their experiments mentors.

Creator’s Camp

E-Summit'24's Creators Camp is your gateway to transforming your creative passions into a thriving business. Our event is packed with inspiring speakers, contests,and to set you on the path to creative success..

IPL Auction

Ruling with the mighty hammer where the three strikes define the risk taking capability at E-Summit’24. It concreates the decision making skill set of the participants in a simulated team bidding environment.

Stock Trading Simulation

The Stock Trading Simulation offers participants the opportunity to master the art of trading before venturing into the real world, providing an immersive, practical learning experience that's as close to the real deal as it gets.

Crypto Trading Simulation

Crypto Trading Simulation allows participants to experience the thrill of crypto trading without the risk, with practical insights gained through simulated investments in real time.

Keynote Session

Keynote Sessions by accomplished individuals offer a priceless chance to gain insights from success stories. They inspire and invigorate, revealing the wisdom of achievers.
